About Me

Purpose, protection, and progress

Hello, I’m Myesha Shipman.

I am a multidisciplinary professional whose work connects cybersecurity, governance, risk and compliance, legal operations, education, and emergency management.

My experience has taught me to look beyond a single system or policy and consider the people, processes, legal responsibilities, and operational risks that affect an organization as a whole.

I created this portfolio to showcase practical projects, research, policies, coursework, professional experience, and resources that reflect how I approach complex problems: with curiosity, structure, accountability, and a commitment to protecting people and information.

My long-term goal is to continue developing at the intersection of technology, compliance, business, and law while helping organizations operate securely, responsibly, and with greater resilience.

Cybersecurity Protecting information, systems, and organizational operations.
Risk & Compliance Turning frameworks and requirements into practical controls.
Legal Operations Supporting research, documentation, process improvement, and accountability.
Emergency Management Preparing organizations to respond, recover, and remain resilient.
